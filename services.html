Grout Refresh | Recolour | Restore

Using unique tools and our cutting-edge compounds we can refresh and RECOLOUR your grout, restoring it to a like new condition. This leaves your grout being able to repel dirt, oil, soil, urine, and other contaminants.

This service is completely hassle free, leaves no mess and affordable. Most businesses and homes cannot close off areas for days at a time but with this service you can be back using the area within a matter of hours. We use a range of 9 different coloured compounds. Our compounds chemically bond to the existing grout creating a uniform colour that is perfect for old, tired, and permanently stained grout lines. This is super effective in bathrooms, shower areas, kitchen splash backs, cloakrooms, floor tiles for both domestic and commercial properties. Just about any tiled surface can be restored to like new condition.

There’s no need to re-grout when you can recolour in a fraction of the time at a fraction of the cost with none of the risk.

Shower Glass Restoration

Many areas of the UK suffer from hard water. Over time this frequently leads to staining of surfaces, most noticeably on shower glass.

These limescale deposits and hard water stains can eventually work their way into the glass making cleaning seemingly impossible.

Using a specially formulated compound that has been optimised for precisely this issue. We can polish the limescale away restoring your shower glass to almost new condition.

Silicone Replacement

Silicone sealant in the bathroom or kitchen can often get discoloured over time.

It can get mouldy, particularly if the sealant is damaged or worn away and water is then able to seep through which can cause leaks.

We remove old silicone fully and replace with high grade anti bacterial, water resistant, anti mould silicone. Multiple colour choices are available.

Hydrophobic and Oleophobic Coatings

GroutGleam have an all-in-one industrial strength hydrophobic and oleophobic coating that uniquely works on glass, tiles, and grout.

The coating forms an invisible barrier on the surfaces. You’ll defiantly notice a difference between a coated and non-coated area. Visually you will see water run off the coated area, leaving only with the odd bead of water. You can just wipe clean with a microfiber cloth. The whole coated area will be a low maintenance wipe clean area.

Its commonly known that grout and some tiles are porous and will absorb water, oils, dirt, and grime. Over time this can lead to discolouration and could potentially lead to mould and mildew deposits. These coatings can reduce the time this takes, by protecting the coated area for up to 12 months!
This is typically a bolt-on service.

Full Regrout

A full re-grout is where we remove your existing grout and replace it with new grout.

A full re-grout should be a last resort option due to there is a risk of damage to your existing tiles. Getting an exact matched tile to you existing tile can prove troublesome, especially if they are a few years old or more.
